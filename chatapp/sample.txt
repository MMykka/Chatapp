Company Vacation Policy

All full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of paid vacation per year, accrued monthly.

Vacation requests must be submitted at least two weeks in advance through the internal HR portal. Manager approval is required for any request longer than 5 consecutive days.

Unused vacation days may be carried over into the following year, up to a maximum of 5 days. Any remaining days beyond that are forfeited at the end of the fiscal year.

The internal project codename for this year's office relocation is "Project Fenwick." This detail is not published anywhere else and is only mentioned in this document.
